The Speedster Goal

As the Speedster group, we are designing a finger to move as fast as possible, defined by both maximum top speed and bandwidth.

This required a lightweight, transparent finger...

With this goal in mind, we designed our finger to:

Whack-a-Mole Demo

To demonstrate the Speedster finger's exceptional speed and bandwidth, we developed a real-time Whack-a-Mole demo. As dots appear on a phone screen, the finger presses them with superhuman speed, with an average reaction time as low as 210ms.

This demo is made possible by our custom real-time inverse kinematics and motion planning in ROS 2 and the finger's lightweight, transparent design.
